## Q12 — Viết hướng dẫn đổ xăng tại cây xăng nhanh nhất có thể

For customers who want to get some gas, please follow these instructions step by step:

1. Select gas type (E10, RON-95, RON-92,...)
2. Line up
3. Speak out loud how much money you want to fill (usually fill it up)
4. Open your gas cap
5. Take a look to the price panel
6. Wait for gas fill
7. Close your gas cap
8. Proceed payment
    8.1. If you choose Cash, give him
    8.2. If you choose Internet Banking, step aside.
        8.2.1. Choose payment methods (Card or QR scan)
        8.2.2. Wait for employee to prepare
        8.2.3. Plug your card or scan QR
        8.2.4. Get invoice
9. Leave peacefully

Tips:
- If you fail to do the payment, leave your ID card and get back when you have money.
- Do not use lighters or any open flames during gas filling.
- Use CASH, do not waste people's time.
